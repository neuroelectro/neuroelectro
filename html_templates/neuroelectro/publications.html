NeuroElectro Publications

Publications describing the NeuroElectro resource

  • Brain-wide analysis of electrophysiological diversity yields novel categorization of mammalian neuron types
    Journal of Neurophysiology, June 2015
    Tripathy SJ, Burton SD, Geramita M, Gerkin RC, and Urban NN
    Description: An in-depth analysis of brain-wide electrophysiological diversity using the NeuroElectro dataset, including techniques for experimental condition normalization and database quality control.
  • NeuroElectro: a window to the world's neuron electrophysiology data.
    Frontiers in Neuroinformatics, April 2014
    Tripathy SJ, Savitskaya J, Burton SD, Urban NN, and Gerkin RC
    Description: A methods paper outlining the text-mining and manual curation methodology used to construct the NeuroElectro resource.

How to cite data obtained from NeuroElectro

    The data represented here is made available under a Creative Commons BY-SA license. We ask that you do the following when making use of this data:
  1. Please cite the original publication(s) from which the electrophysiological data were obtained. This helps ensure that the original authors get credit for their work.
  2. We also ask that you cite one of the NeuroElectro-specific publications listed above and refer to the NeuroElectro.org web URL within your publication.
